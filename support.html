Support

If you hit a bug or need licensing help, include your extension version, browser version, and a short reproduction path.

Support channels

  • GitHub issues: Open a support ticket

Fast troubleshooting checklist

  1. Refresh the Genspark tab and retry export.
  2. Open extension settings and revalidate your license key.
  3. Confirm checkout URLs are configured and the license validation endpoint is reachable.
  4. If the issue persists, open a GitHub issue with your browser version and steps to reproduce.
